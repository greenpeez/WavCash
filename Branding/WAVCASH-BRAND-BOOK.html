02 / Logo
The Diffraction.
A single-slit diffraction pattern: a bold central bar flanked by progressively thinner, fainter bars. This is how sound and light behave when they pass through an opening. The invisible, made visible. Inspired by wave optics, directly echoing the tagline.

Mercury WebGL.
